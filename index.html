Hi, I'm Liam

I am a 19-year-old programmer currently studying Games Programming at Staffordshire University, passionate about low-level games programming.

From a young age, playing games on my dad's PC, I was always curious about how things worked; how you could place monkeys in Bloons Tower Defense 5, or how the world of Minecraft seemed to go on forever. That curiosity drives my projects and fuels my interest in building efficient scalable systems

Outside of programming, I enjoy gaming; having over 4000 hours on steam alone, spending most my time in competitive shooters, factory sims and the occasional game of pool or darts with friends and family. Additionally i build PC's for myself and friends and love to cook.
