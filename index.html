Hi, Im Liam

I am a 19 year old programmer currently studying Games Programming at Staffordshire University. Before that i studied IT at Bournemouth and Poole College.

From a young age, playing games on my dad's PC, I was always curios how things worked - How you could place the monkeys in Bloons Tower Defense 5, or how the world of Minecraft was made to seem to go on forever. This curiosity later developed into a passion, recreating such mechanics, experimenting with ways to improve them.

Ever since joining my school's code club in year 5, i have explored a wide range of technologies. Starting from scratch and Kodu, then moving into more traditional text-based languages like python and C#, and eventually finding my passion in C++ which is now my go-to for most projects. I have covered a wide variety of areas of programming and now want to refine my skills to create cleaner, and more effective code.
